PROJECT 4:
The Safety Standoff: Anthropic, the Pentagon, and Military AI
https://kevinlu4588.github.io/llm-war-explainer/

TOPIC OVERVIEW:

My synthesis report covered the Anthropic-Pentagon dispute of February-March 2026. The most surprising finding was that the Pentagon blacklisted Anthropic for refusing to remove two safety restrictions, then hours later signed a deal with OpenAI that included essentially the same restrictions. That contradiction suggests the dispute was never about the safeguards themselves but about who gets to set conditions when the government buys AI. For this project, I want a general audience to understand the timeline, the legal stakes, and why federal legislation on military AI is needed.

PROPOSED GENRE/PLATFORM:

Interactive editorial website, modeled after long-form visual storytelling pieces from outlets like The Verge or NYT interactive. The site includes an interactive timeline, side-by-side comparison graphics, a legal flowchart, and a UN vote visualization to make the story more accessible and engaging than a traditional essay.

AUDIENCE:

General public, especially people who follow tech news but haven't looked closely at the details of this dispute. The kind of reader who saw the headlines about Anthropic vs. the Pentagon but doesn't know that OpenAI got the same safeguards days later, or that a federal judge called the government's actions "Orwellian."

CITATION STYLE FOR THE REPORT:

Informal in-text attribution woven into the prose, with a full references section at the bottom of the page listing all 10 sources in APA format.

WORK PLAN:

Week 1: Identify the key narrative threads from the synthesis report and outline the sections and interactive figures for the site.

Week 2: Build the website structure, write all prose content, and implement the four interactive visualizations (timeline, comparison panel, legal flowchart, UN vote waffle chart).

Remaining time: Polish visual design, test interactivity, add dark mode and accessibility features, revise prose for a general audience.
